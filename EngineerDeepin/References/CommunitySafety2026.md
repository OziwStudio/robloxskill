# Community Safety 2026

## Purpose

- Use for moderation, social features, chat, ads, UGC, youth audience, subscriptions, and publish readiness.
- Treat 2026 policy details as `Verify-Current` unless user provides official current source.
- Never bypass Roblox safety systems.

## Age And Access

- Check target audience before enabling social or UGC systems.
- Younger audiences need stricter content, chat, voice, ads, and moderation assumptions.
- Social hangouts, free-draw, and sensitive topics need extra policy caution.
- If parental approval or age-gated access is relevant, state assumptions and verify current Roblox rules.

## Chat And Voice

- Prefer Roblox safety-filtered chat systems.
- Do not build custom unfiltered chat.
- Treat age group APIs, voice grouping, trusted connection APIs, and chat timeout behavior as `Verify-Current`.
- Keep moderation reporting path visible and non-blocking.

## UGC And AI Data

- Do not promise private handling for assets uploaded to Roblox unless verified by current ToS.
- For user-generated content, require moderation before display when risk exists.
- For AI or external tools, do not send private project data without user approval.

## Ads And Monetization

- Avoid deceptive prompts.
- Keep personalized ads and underage audiences policy-aware.
- Treat recurring purchases, battle pass, and subscription terms as policy-sensitive.
- Receipt and entitlement logic stays server-side.

## Publish Gate

- Target age rating identified.
- Chat and voice behavior reviewed.
- UGC path reviewed.
- Ads and monetization reviewed.
- Reporting flow reviewed.
- Current policy assumptions marked.
